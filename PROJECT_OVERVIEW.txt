========================================================================
NIT DEM: TRAFFIC DENSITY AND DEVIATION MONITORING SYSTEM - PROJECT OVERVIEW
========================================================================

1. INTRODUCTION & OBJECTIVE
---------------------------
The NIT DEM (Traffic Density and Deviation Monitor) is a state-of-the-art real-time traffic monitoring, forecasting, and incident response dashboard designed for Kozhikode's urban road network (comprising 10 primary junctions and 24 connecting links). 

The platform enables traffic management operators to:
* Visualize live road traffic telemetry (Speed, Volume, Density, Travel Times).
* Inspect 20-minute predictive bottlenecks forecast by a Spatio-Temporal Graph Neural Network (ST-GNN) model.
* Run dynamic "What-If" simulation adjustments (lane closures, event severity, signal retiming offsets) to evaluate network recovery.
* Dispatch and track UAV (drone) surveillance flights to verify real-time incident conditions.

---

2. WEB INTERFACE: FUNCTIONAL MANUAL (SECTION BY SECTION)
--------------------------------------------------------

SECTION A: THE HEADER & NAVIGATION BAR (TOP ROW)
* Page Tabs: Switch between the 4 primary views of the system:
  1. Main Map (Operational dashboard)
  2. Incident Center (Validation feed)
  3. Drone Operations (UAV flight panel)
  4. AI Analytics (Historical reports)
* Role Selector: Toggle between Operator Levels (Supervisor L3, Officer L2, Dispatcher L1) to check permission-based incident validations.
* Live Status Indicator: Displays network online health, live local system time, and current active alert counters.

SECTION B: THE MAIN OPERATIONAL MAP (CENTER VIEW)
* Interactive Road Connections: Renders 24 road segments, colored dynamically based on current congestion levels:
  - Green (Free Flow): Under 35% density, traffic flowing normally.
  - Orange (Moderate): 35% - 55% density, slight buildup.
  - Red (Heavy): 55% - 70% density, significant delays.
  - Dark Red (Critical): Over 70% density, severe congestion.
* Bidirectional Hover Tooltips: Hovering over any road segment displays a stacked popup detailing metrics for both link directions separately (e.g. Bus Stand Junction ➔ Arayidathupalam Junction vs. the reverse direction).
* Active Incident Pins: Flashing alert markers showing locations of logged accidents or blocks. Clicking on a marker displays the incident details card.
* UAV Drone Icons: Pinned icons representing the real-time flight path coordinates of active drones.

SECTION C: TEMPORAL CONTROL BAR (BOTTOM TIMELINE SEEKER)
* Time Scrubber (Range Slider): Seek to any precise minute of the day (00:00:00 to 23:59:59) to playback historical traffic telemetry or review corresponding forecast points.
* Playback Loop Controls: Play, pause, step forward, or step backward through the timeline.
* Playback Speed: Accelerate playback speed (1x, 5x, 15x, 30x) to run fast-forward network reviews.

SECTION D: INTELLIGENT CENTER (RIGHT SIDEBAR DRAWER)
* Tab 1: Live Telemetry Overview
  - Displays a detailed 3x3 stats card of current traffic metrics (Volume, Speed, Density, Travel Time, Queue length) for a selected link segment.
* Tab 2: 20-Min Forecast Panel
  - Displays AI bottleneck alerts and recommended dynamic strategies (e.g., "Reroute via Mini Bypass", "Increase green light phase by 18s") generated by the ST-GNN forecasting model.
* Tab 3: What-If Simulation Sandbox
  - A sandbox panel where operators can adjust sliders (Lanes Blocked, Event Intensity, Signal Retiming offsets) to preview predicted impacts on congestion and travel times instantly.
* Tab 4: Drone Telemetry Feed
  - Click on any active drone to open a live video preview feed overlay, showing the drone's coordinates, battery percentage, altitude, and current flight logs.

SECTION E: INCIDENT CENTER FEED & AI RANKINGS (FULL SCREEN VIEW)
* Active Incident Feed: List of all current logs, showing coordinates, description, and status. Pending incidents show L3 validation buttons ("Accept" or "Decline" which require Supervisor credentials).
* AI Disruption Rankings Tab: An advanced diagnostic panel that dynamically scores and ranks all logged incidents from most disruptive (#1) to least disruptive. The rank is calculated based on priority level, lane closures, and junction density.

SECTION F: DRONE OPERATIONS PANEL (FULL SCREEN VIEW)
* Active Flight Paths: Separate Leaflet map plotting active drone coordinates, flight routes, and home bases.
* UAV Statistics Grid: Live cards tracking drone batteries, signal strength, and airspeed.
* Manual Dispatch Controls: Form to select a target junction, assign a drone flight route, and dispatch a UAV automatically.

SECTION G: AI ANALYTICS & DIAGNOSTICS PAGE (FULL SCREEN VIEW)
* Historical Deviation Charts: Line charts plotting actual traffic density vs. ST-GNN forecast trends over the past 24 hours.
* Data Sync Logger: Telemetry ingestion logs tracking CSV updates from Google Cloud Storage.
